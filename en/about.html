Creativity

The ability to see across scientific boundaries and produce consistent value, through hard work of technical and creative quality, provides a unique leverage to any endeavor. As someone who came from a uniquely diverse scientific background, in Academic Philosophy and Computer Science, I am a firm believer in the power of creative, multidisciplinary approaches to problem solving, system creation, and innovation.

Focus

The majority of knowledge work achievements demand solitary and focused commitment to a goal and its process, but they do often require organized, consistent teamwork, to put together and bring synergy to any project and produce excellent output.

Enthusiasm

Feeling the accomplishment and value of great work may be one of the most gratifying experiences, and it requires a certain degree of enthusiasm that is driven, not only by the process, but also by the goal.
